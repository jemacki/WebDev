About Mac Alterixx

Mac Alterixx is passionate about exploring creativity through furniture, interior design, and fashion. I love how these worlds intersect — how the textures, shapes, and styles of everyday life can tell a story. Inspired heavily by designers like Rick Owens and Sruli Recht, I is drawn to bold, experimental aesthetics that mix minimalism with unique detail. These details really bring in a unique sense of style into your home; Breathe taking


For me, furniture isn’t just functional, it’s an expression. Interior Spaces aren’t just places to Live, they’re reflections of personality. And fashion isn’t just clothing, it’s a way to present an identity to the world. These interests are my creative playground, forever in my thoughts — a place where inspiration, style, and personal passion meet.
